About us

About owner

Hello, and welcome to the Los Pollos Hermanos family. My name is Gustavo, but you can call me Gus. I am thrilled that you'll be joining our team. Each and every day we serve our customers exceptional items, with impeccable service. We take pride in everything that we do.

About our restaurants

Experience the epitome of culinary excellence at Los Pollos Hermanos, where we blend our passion for great food with unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction. From the moment you step foot into our establishment, you will be enveloped in an atmosphere that exudes sophistication and hospitality.

Not just a place to eat

Los Pollos Hermanos is not your ordinary restaurant. It is an embodiment of Gustavo Fring's impeccable standards and attention to detail. Every corner of this establishment has been meticulously designed to cater to the discerning palates and refined tastes of our esteemed clientele.

Menu

Our menu is a testament to the harmonious fusion of traditional family recipes and cutting-edge culinary techniques. Whether you crave the succulent, flame-grilled chicken or the indulgent, handcrafted tortas, each dish is carefully crafted to epitomize perfection.
